b.luntly

Can you handle the truth... or will it handle you?

We can all improve something about ourselves. The first step towards that is asking the right questions. Or more simply, maybe you are just curious about how others truly see you.

b.luntly is a tool for getting anonymous and truthful answers to questions.

Ask Questions

  • What kind of first impression do you make?
  • Does your self-image reflect what others think?
  • How did you perform on your job interview?

Get Answers

  • Answers are anonymous.
  • Can be brutally honest.
  • Offer you an opportunity to improve.
